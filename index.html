Mancala

Game Rules

Each player has a store and some pits where they will “sow” the seeds.

On a turn, a player picks up all the seeds in one pit and “sows” them counter clockwise, placing one seed in each of the pits along the way. If you come to your store, then add a seed to your store and continue. You may end up putting seeds in your opponent's pits along the way but never on it's store.

If the last seed falls within the player own store, then he can play again

If the last seed falls on your own pit, all the seeds in the opposing pit go to your store

The game is over when one player's pits are completely empty. The other player takes the seeds remaining in her pits and puts those seeds in her store. The player with the most seeds on its store wins.
